What is 1kg of CO₂?

Carbon dioxide is an invisible gas, making it hard to visualize its physical impact on the climate. In STRATA, we measure your footprint in exact kilograms. To put 1 kg of CO₂ into perspective, it is mathematically equivalent to:

🚗 Driving a standard petrol car for ~4.0 km
📱 Fully charging a smartphone ~122 times
🔥 Burning solid coal ~0.4 kg
🌱

How Activities Reduce CO₂

When you log an action in STRATA, you are offsetting your emissions. Here is exactly how much you save and why:

🚲 Cycling Commute

Saves 0.2 kg CO₂ per km

By choosing to cycle instead of driving a fossil-fuel vehicle, you directly prevent tailpipe emissions from entering the atmosphere. The 0.2kg metric represents the average emission of a standard ICE (Internal Combustion Engine) vehicle per kilometer.

🥗 Plant-Based Meal

Saves 1.5 kg CO₂ per meal

Meat production (especially beef) requires massive amounts of land, water, and generates high methane emissions. Swapping one heavy meat meal for a vegan alternative reduces supply-chain emissions by approximately 1.5kg.

♻️ E-Waste Recycling

Saves 1.4 kg CO₂ per kg

Mining raw rare-earth metals for electronics is extremely carbon-intensive. By safely recycling e-waste, you reintroduce processed materials into the supply chain, averting 1.4kg of mining emissions per kg recycled.

System Formulas

STRATA's ledger engine relies on exact mathematical formulas to compute your Net Score dynamically.

1. The Net Score Equation

NetScore = Generated - (Reduced + Restored)

2. Baseline Generation Rate

Generated = 1.2 kg * DaysElapsed

Assuming a conservative base generation of 1.2kg/day for a standard user before specific footprint surveys.

3. Investment Restorations (Trees)

Restored = (Trees * 10.0) / Month

Used in the Green Action Allocator. A mature tree offsets exactly 10kg per month.

Technical Alignment

A high-level blueprint of the APIs, logic nodes, and infrastructure wiring that power STRATA's real-time capabilities.

Firebase Backend

Auth & Firestore DB

Provides secure user authentication. Firestore securely synchronizes the user's `Generated`, `Reduced`, and `Restored` metrics to the cloud in real-time.

Gemini 1.5 Flash AI Engine

REST API • Multimodal • NLP

Google's cutting-edge AI powers two critical nodes:
1. Lithos AI Coach (Context-aware personalized text chat)
2. NECTRRA Vision (Real-time Base64 image ingestion for waste classification and treatment JSON generation).

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API

Geospatial Radar Querying

Used by the "Eco Hubs Near You" system. Dynamically queries coordinates for NWR (nodes, ways, relations) tags like `shop=florist`, `amenity=recycling`, or `office=ngo` within a 5km radius to guide local action.

Open-Meteo & Nominatim API

Environment Metrics

Uses GPS to hit Open-Meteo for real-time European AQI, while Nominatim performs reverse-geocoding to translate exact latitude/longitude into human-readable city localities.
